About Us

Bethany’s baking journey began at a roadside pie stand, where the irresistible aroma of her hand-crafted, small-batch pies would draw in passersby from miles away. Her secret? Farm-fresh ingredients, flaky all-butter crusts, and recipes passed down through generations. In 1986, fueled by a growing fanbase and a deep love for honest baking, she opened the first Bethany’s Pie Shop on Main Street—where community and comfort came together, one slice at a time.

Today, Bethany’s Pie Shop remains proudly family-owned and operated by her granddaughters, Alicia and Sarah. They bake every pie from scratch each morning, using locally sourced produce, organic flour, and Bethany’s original time-honored recipes. Every pie is shipped overnight to guarantee homemade freshness and that just-baked taste—because for Alicia and Sarah, pie isn’t just dessert, it’s a way of life.
